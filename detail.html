Defense Day

Hero image — the moment of defense

On 14 January 2026 I defended Co-Exploration in Remote Design Collaboration at Eindhoven University of Technology. The thesis asks how tools can better support the way design teams build on one another's ideas — especially at a distance — and brings together the publications from across my doctorate.

It proceeds in four moves: envisioning future remote-prototyping tools, mapping the limits of current ones, defining and characterising co-exploration through a five-month study, and translating all of it into a practical guideline for designers.

Behind the scenes

I designed the book myself — the cover, the illustrations, and the writing — so that the object would feel like the argument. The defense itself was a real ceremony: a committee drawn from universities across the world, and a university news post to mark it.

What stays with me most is the people in the room. The PhD is often called a lonely journey, but mine was accompanied at every turn by supervisors, colleagues, friends, and family.
