Longitudinal Observation

61 participants · 16 teams · 5 months · mixed-method · project management

The heart of the thesis. I coordinated 16 teams and 61 participants over five months — scheduling, briefing, keeping everyone motivated through a demanding process — using a weekly diary study to capture how design activities really unfold. Rich qualitative data became a clear four-dimensional framework, and I held 100% participant engagement from start to finish.

A Study Born from Crisis

COVID-19 · problem-sensitive · rapid research

When the pandemic forced design teams into remote work overnight, I noticed the struggle and moved fast — designing two studies to capture the moment through interviews and a survey. The finding that shaped everything after: remote work quietly eroded co-exploration, and more meetings didn't bring it back.
